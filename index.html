Privacy Policy – AR Book App

AR Book App uses your device's camera to provide augmented reality (AR) features. These features enhance your experience by overlaying 3D content onto real-world book pages.

The app does not collect, store, or share any camera data. All processing is done locally on your device.

We do not collect personal information, and your privacy is fully respected.
