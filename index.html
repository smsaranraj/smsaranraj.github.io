Privacy Policy – AR Book App

AR Book App uses your device's camera to deliver augmented reality (AR) features by overlaying 3D content on physical book pages.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any camera data to our servers or third parties. The camera is used solely to recognize visual patterns in real time and render AR content locally on your device.

We respect your privacy and do not collect personal information without your consent.
